Self Study 1

With the first semester of the last year finished the 2 week break has begun. I am very close to graduating, only 2 more electives to complete. Not entirely sure what electives I am going to choose as of yet, but I do know that I want to get into a good routine now that my bachelor degree is nearing the end. I have created a daily schedule which I am going to follow for ideally the rest of my life that I believe will be utilizing the most of the time I have.

The daily schedule is as follows …

* 6:00 am
    - Wake up
    - Shower
    - Brush teeth
    - Get dressed
    - Check emails

* 6:15 am
    - Blog

* 7:00 am
    - Work

* 5:30 pm
    - Make/eat dinner

* 7:00 pm 
    - Study

* 9:00 pm
    - Work on a personal project

* 11:00 pm
    - Filler (anything)

* 12:00 am
    - Sleep

This schedule allows for me to blog, work 10 hours, study for 2 hours, work on a personal project for 2 hours, an hour for personal time, and 6 hours for sleep.

I am normally quite bad at planning, let alone following through with a plan, so this is more of a proposed daily schedule. There will be things that come up which will take away time for others, but overall the idea is to follow when possible.

This blog is my first step towards this schedule.
