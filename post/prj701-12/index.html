Project 701 ~ The Future

The project is now coming to an end, and I find myself writing my last blog which I am titling “The Future” to discuss my plans from here. Now that I know the basics of Odoo and how to deploy an instance, I will most certainly be using Odoo for the infrastructure of all my personal business ventures (like Ponte Posterum). Krupesh has offered me a permanent position at Venus, so I will be continuing with him and helping to build some amazing stuff. My learnings in Odoo will continue, both in the use of the software suite and the development of it. Along with learning Odoo, I will also be embarking in client aquisitions to start building the client base for Venus. This is why I am using the poster night to be the basis for my pitch to clients; essentially killing 2 birds with 1 stone. My learning in general will also continue, primarily in IT, but I do plan on studying some other subjects (like choas theory). I would eventually like to attain my masters in data science, but before that I will spend a couple years studying statistics. Traveling is also one of the things I planned to do, not entirely sure where yet (I do want to visit Japan and my homeland Norway).
